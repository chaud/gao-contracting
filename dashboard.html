              What is the trend in spending through Other Transaction Agreements?

              OTAs are a flexible alternative to traditional contracts. These figures are not included in the $793B total above.

              Insight

              OTA spending reached $18.2B in FY2025 — down $700M from FY2024 but still well above pre-2023 levels. The Army and Air Force dominate the category.
